# 🎓 HMG ACADEMY CLASS DECK (v8) — Quick User Guide

Print this or keep it open during your first classes.

---

## FOR THE TEACHER — Mode 1: Google Meet / Zoom / any conferencing app

1. Open **Google Meet, Zoom, Teams, Jitsi or WhatsApp video**, start your class, admit students.
2. In the app: **Share screen → Share entire screen → Start sharing**.
3. Press recent-apps and open **ClassDeck → Teach on Google Meet**
   (green badge appears; the call keeps running in the background).
4. Left pane = ✏ Whiteboard, right pane = 📄 PDF (open today's material, or 📚
   load a prepared lesson deck).
5. Tap **🎯 Focus** — all toolbars AND the browser address bar hide (true full
   screen); this clean screen is what Meet/Zoom shares.
   - Want students to see YOU? Tap **📷 Cam** — your face floats on screen
     (drag it anywhere, tap it to resize) and rides along in the screen share.
   - Want to see THEM? Tap **📺 See students** for three free ways (Meet/Zoom
     PiP over this app, a second phone, or the ClassDeck hybrid camera grid).
   - Recording here (⏺) **cannot** break the Meet/Zoom share — it uses its own
     private pipeline.
   - Floating capsule (top-left): pen 🖍 highlighter 🧽 eraser 🔴 laser, ↩ undo,
     ‹ › board pages, ＋ new page, ◫ layout.
   - ☰ brings the full toolbars back; tap 🎯 again to re-hide.
6. PDF tricks: **✏ Annotate** to draw on the page (🧹 clears), tap Annotate
   again to scroll/zoom; drag the centre divider to resize panes
   (double-tap divider = back to 50/50).
7. End: switch back to Meet → stop sharing → end call.

**If Meet ever drops:** ClassDeck is unaffected — rejoin Meet, share again,
and your boards are exactly where you left them (autosave).

## FOR THE TEACHER — Mode 2: Built-in live classroom (no Meet)

### Before class
1. Launch **ClassDeck** from your home screen (or open the URL in Chrome).
2. Tap **Open Teacher Studio**.
3. Set up your panes:
   - Left tab **✏ Whiteboard** (default).
   - Right tab **📄 PDF** → **Open PDF** → pick today's material.
4. Optional: ⚙ Settings → set your name and a class name students will see.

### Starting the class
1. **▶ Go Live** → allow the **microphone** (students need to hear you!).
2. **🔗 Invite** → **Copy** → paste the link into your class WhatsApp/Telegram
   group (or show the QR code for students in the room).
3. Wait for the 👥 counter to rise as students join.
4. Optional: **📷 Cam** so students see your face; **⏺ Rec** to record the lesson.

### Ending the class
1. **⏹ End** (confirms first).
2. 👥 → **📋 Attendance CSV** to save the register.
3. If you recorded: the `.webm` video is already in your Downloads.

### If something goes wrong
- **Students can't hear you** → tap 🎙 Mic; check Chrome's mic permission.
- **A student sees a black screen** → tell them to tap the
  “▶ Start class view” button (their browser needs one tap to allow audio/video).
- **Network blip** → both sides auto-reconnect; if a student drops, they just
  reopen the same link.
- **Screen looks cramped** → ⛶ fullscreen, or ◫ to give one pane full width.

## FOR STUDENTS (share this part with your class)

1. Open the link your teacher sent (any phone, tablet or laptop — no app, no account).
2. Type the **room code** (already filled if you used the link) and **your name** → **Join class**.
3. If asked, tap **“▶ Start class view”** once — that's your browser asking
   permission to play sound.
4. You now see the teacher's full screen, live. Rotate your phone sideways for
   the biggest view. The teacher's face appears in a small window you can drag.
5. Your buttons (tap the screen if they hide):
   - ✋ raise your hand
   - 💬 ask a question in chat
   - 📷 share your camera (only when you agree)
   - 🎙 speak — works only after the teacher allows your mic
   - ⛶ fullscreen 🚪 leave
6. When a 📊 poll appears, tap your answer.
7. When a 🏆 quiz appears: answer before the timer runs out — faster correct
   answers earn more points. You'll see ✅/❌ instantly and the top-10
   leaderboard at the end.
8. If your teacher set a **Class PIN**, type it on the join screen (ask your
   teacher if you don't have it).
9. **Early?** Join anyway — you'll wait in the lobby and enter automatically
   the moment your teacher goes live. Keep the page open.
10. **Show your work:** tap 🖥 to share your screen with the teacher (only the
    teacher sees it). Tap again to stop.
11. **Private question?** Tick "send privately to teacher only" in the chat.
12. **Connection lost?** Stay on the page — a yellow banner shows while it
    reconnects you automatically (it keeps trying for up to 10 minutes).

**Data-saving tip:** the class video is optimised for low data (~150–300 MB per
hour at default quality). Wi-Fi is still cheaper than mobile data!

# v9 Addendum — Screen Sharing, Social Live, Captions and Noise Meter

## A. Share your full screen directly inside ClassDeck

1. Open `teach.html`.
2. Tap **⚙ Settings**.
3. Under **Broadcast mode**, choose **Share screen**.
4. Tap **Save**.
5. Tap **▶ Go Live**.
6. Your browser will ask what to share. Choose your whole screen, a window, or a tab.
7. Students join with the normal ClassDeck link/room code; they do not need Google Meet or Zoom.

If screen sharing is not supported or permission is denied, ClassDeck automatically falls back to Composite mode.

## B. Stream your class to social media with free OBS

1. Open **⚙ Settings → 📡 Open centre**, or visit `stream.html`.
2. Download the OBS setup plan.
3. In OBS Studio, add a **Window Capture** source and select the ClassDeck browser window.
4. Go to OBS **Settings → Stream**.
5. Paste the RTMP URL and stream key from YouTube, Facebook, TikTok, Instagram Live Producer, or another platform.
6. Click **Start Streaming** in OBS.

Important: ClassDeck never stores stream keys. Keep them in OBS or the social platform dashboard.

## C. Live captions

1. Go live.
2. Open the **💬 Chat** drawer.
3. Tap **CC Live captions**.
4. Speak clearly. Students see captions in a bottom banner.
5. Tap **⬇ Transcript** to download the caption transcript.

This uses your browser's speech recognition only. No AI API key is required.

## D. Noise meter

1. Open the **👥 Students** drawer.
2. Tap **🔊 Noise meter**.
3. Tap **Start meter** and allow microphone permission.
4. Adjust the threshold slider.
5. When active, the noise gauge appears in the broadcast overlay so learners can self-regulate.

Nothing is recorded or uploaded by the noise meter.

# ClassDesk v2 Addendum — Direct Tablet Screen and No-OBS Social Live

## 1. Share your ClassDeck screen directly to students

1. Open `teach.html`.
2. Tap **▶ Go Live**.
3. Students join with the normal room code/link.
4. They see the ClassDeck teaching workspace directly — no Google Meet or Zoom required.

## 2. Try full tablet screen sharing

1. Open **⚙ Settings**.
2. Tap **🖥 Try full tablet screen**.
3. If your tablet browser supports full screen capture, choose what to share.
4. If it does not, ClassDeck will continue with workspace capture.

## 3. Stream to social media from tablet without OBS

1. Deploy the included relay: `relay/no-obs-social-relay/`.
2. Open `teach.html` on your tablet.
3. Open **⚙ Settings → 📡 Tablet Live**.
4. Enter your relay gateway URL and secret.
5. Paste full RTMP/RTMPS publish URLs from YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok or custom destination.
6. Choose:
   - Landscape 16:9 for YouTube/Facebook.
   - Vertical 9:16 for TikTok/Instagram.
7. Tap **📡 Start tablet social live**.

## 4. If you do not have the relay yet

Open `stream.html`. It explains the no-OBS workflow and links to browser-based live studios. These options depend on each provider's free plan and your account eligibility.

# ClassDesk v3 Addendum — Security and Picture-in-Picture

## A. Use Picture-in-Picture while teaching

1. Open Teacher Studio.
2. Start your class or prepare your board.
3. Tap **▣ PiP** in the top toolbar.
4. Keep the floating PiP window open while switching/minimising.

Note: browsers require you to tap the PiP button yourself. A website cannot force PiP automatically after you minimise.

## B. Secure invite links

1. Open **⚙ Settings**.
2. Tick **Require secure invite link token**.
3. Save.
4. Share the invite link from **🔗 Invite**.

Students must use the link. Room-code-only entry will be rejected while this option is on.

## C. Forensic watermark

1. Open **⚙ Settings**.
2. Keep **Add forensic watermark** enabled.
3. Live broadcasts and recordings are stamped faintly with teacher/room information.

This discourages unauthorised recording or resale.

## D. Export security audit log

1. Open **👥 Students** drawer.
2. Tap **🛡 Audit CSV**.
3. Save the CSV for records.

## E. Subscription protection

For strongest subscription protection, the platform owner should deploy the Cloudflare Worker gateway in:

```text
security/license-gateway-worker/
```

Then set the Worker URL in:

```text
js/security-config.js
```
