📡 Tablet Social Live — No OBS

Broadcast a live ClassDeck lesson from your tablet to YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok or a custom RTMP destination. The built-in path is ClassDeck tablet → WebRTC relay → social RTMP platforms.

1. Direct from tablet: recommended workflow

  1. Deploy the included relay folder: relay/no-obs-social-relay/ on a free/low-cost VM with HTTPS.
  2. Open Teacher Studio on your tablet.
  3. Open ⚙ Settings → 📡 Tablet Live.
  4. Paste your relay URL and the social platform RTMP/RTMPS publish URLs.
  5. Tap Start tablet social live. ClassDeck sends the lesson canvas + mic to the relay; the relay publishes to your platforms.
Why a relay is needed: the modern web browser can send WebRTC, but social platforms still commonly require RTMP/RTMPS ingest. The relay converts WebRTC to RTMP without needing OBS on the tablet.

2. Full tablet screen vs ClassDeck screen

On many Android tablets, browser security does not expose full-system screen capture to websites. ClassDeck therefore provides two routes:

  • ClassDeck workspace capture: always available because the app captures its own teaching canvas.
  • Full tablet screen attempt: available only if the tablet/browser supports getDisplayMedia.
If full Android screen capture is not available, your students/social audience still see the whole ClassDeck teaching workspace — whiteboard, PDFs, browser cast, notes, toolkit, camera, captions and overlays.

3. Social platform notes

▶ YouTube

Use YouTube Studio/Live Control Room to create a stream and copy the RTMP server + key. Paste the full publish URL into Tablet Live.

f Facebook

Use Facebook Live Producer. Copy the secure RTMPS publish URL/key. Some workflows require you to click Go Live in Facebook after the preview appears.

📸 Instagram

Use a professional/creator account with Instagram Live Producer. Keys are temporary and usually must be generated for each live session.

🎵 TikTok

TikTok Live/RTMP access depends on account eligibility or Live Center access. Use the RTMP URL/key shown by TikTok when available.

Protect stream keys: do not commit keys to GitHub. Enter them only on your own tablet and relay over HTTPS.
