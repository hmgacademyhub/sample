📺 Seeing your students while teaching here

While Google Meet / Zoom shares your screen, you have three free ways to keep an eye on students:

  1. Meet picture-in-picture: in Google Meet (Android), tap Home while in the call — Meet shrinks to a floating window showing participants on top of this app. Drag it to a corner. (Zoom: enable Settings → Meeting → Show my video in PiP.)
  2. Split second device: join the Meet/Zoom call muted on your phone and lay it beside the tablet as a dedicated "student monitor".
  3. ClassDeck hybrid: have students ALSO open your ClassDeck room link (no audio needed) and share their cameras here — open the 👥 drawer to see the camera grid while Meet handles audio/screen.

Tip: your own camera → tap 📷 Cam. Your face appears in a floating window on this screen, so it is included in the screen share and students see you. Drag it anywhere; tap it to cycle size.
